Theme Park

Fun & Feast is Gujarat’s first theme park based on the iconic Kids game and movie located at Gandhinagar City. With everything from educational activities for youngsters, to full-sized exciting rides for the adults, there's hours of fun waiting for the entire family! More than 35 rides span across 1000 sq m in this combination indoor and outdoor park. There are many restaurants and kiosks (which can be rented) inside the park where you can enjoy various types of meals, snacks, and drinks available to all.

In short, this amusement park boasts all facilities --making it a one-stop destination for people of all ages. You want to set up your theme park or partner with us? then lets connect, submit the contact us form and our representative will connect with you.
